Services

 

Television

Host and Video Disc Jockey (VDJ)

DJ Bash has experience as a TV host & Video Disc Jockey having worked for NTV - a leading Kenyan television station - on a weekly teen entertainment show, Teen Republik for five years. 

 
 

Radio

Homeboyz Radio 103.5 FM 

DJ Bash hosts a radio show, The Juice In The Mix, on Homeboyz Radio 103.5 FM every Friday night from 9:00pm - 11:00pm. A show that he records and posts on his YouTube Channel - DJ Bash Kenya. 

 

Concerts

DJ Bash has performed in some of the biggest Kenya concerts

DJ Bash has performed in some of the biggest concerts in Kenya. Having performed alongside some international acts such as, 2 Chainz, Ty Dolla Sign, Stefflon Don, Nasty C and many more. 

Clubs and BarsClub Performances and Appearance

DJ Bash aslo offers entertainment at night clubs having performed in most of the top clubs in Kenya.

DJ Services for Corporates

DJ Bash has provided DJ services to some top corporate companies in Kenya.
